Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa, known as the "Giant of Africa" due to its large population and economy. It's the most populous country in Africa with over 200 million people. Nigeria is rich in cultural diversity with over 250 ethnic groups, and it's famous for its music (Afrobeats), Nollywood film industry, and diverse cuisine like jollof rice and suya.
